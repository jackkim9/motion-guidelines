Introduction

Motion and animation adds an extra dimension to digital design by adding physicality to otherwise 2-dimensional UI elements. Cohesive rhythm of motion and animation makes digital touch-points feel as sophisticated and delightful as the ones in the real world.

Duration

Duration defines how fast an element in moving in the user interface. In the physical world, speed of an object is defined by weight and friction. Heavier objects take longer time to move around. Similarly, in the digial interface, large movements should take longer to move. Objects that convey large amount of information such as an error message should also transition slowly to give sufficient amount of time for the users to digest the information throughly.
